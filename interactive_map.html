Interactive Map

Hover over any county with a mouse or hold your finger on a county to see the latest Coronavirus figures for that county. To zoom use the scroll wheel of the mouse, double click / double tap or use the '+' and '-' buttons. To pan click and drag / hold and drag. Please take note of the "Last Updated" information at the top right of the page to see when the data was last updated.
